Jr. NTR

Jr. NTR (Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr.) is a well-known Indian film actor who works primarily in Telugu cinema. He has earned acclaim for his powerful performances and versatile acting skills.

Birth Details

Jr. NTR was born on May 20, 1983, in Hyderabad, India. He is the grandson of the esteemed actor and politician Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (NTR).
